About us

Yuccess Nigeria Ltd. is an agro-processing, commodity trading, and export company on a mission to bolster national food security and international trade. Since our founding in 2019, we’ve specialized in sourcing, milling, packaging, and exporting premium table rice—our flagship Dada Rice—that meets international standards. We also facilitate the export of diverse agricultural produce, including garlic, hibiscus flour, soya beans, sesame seed, maize, millet, sorghum, and Arabic gum. By deeply engaging with smallholder farmers (especially women) and partnering with agro-dealers, we provide reliable offtake solutions and modern best practices across the value chain.

Vision

To be Nigeria’s premier processor, trader, and exporter of rice and agricultural commodities, providing seamless access to world-class products and services throughout the agro-commodities value chain.

Mission

• Deliver exceptional value to customers through commodity production and export
• Operate profitably while leading core domestic and export markets
• Make quality commodities accessible locally and globally

Impact

We align our work with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals:

• Decent Work & Economic Growth (SDG 8): Creating fair, profitable livelihoods for all value-chain actors
